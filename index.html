We develop platforms for large-scale genome engineering in primary human cells, with a focus on hematopoietic stem cell gene therapy and CAR-T immunotherapy. Our work combines CRISPR and prime editing with high-throughput approaches to build curative cell-based medicines.

The lab is based at DTU Health Tech in Kgs. Lyngby, Denmark.

People

  • CAR-T cell engineering and functional genomics screens
