Join

We are a young and growing lab at DTU Health Tech working at the interface of genome engineering, stem cell biology, and cell therapy. We are looking for enthusiastic and rigorous scientists who are excited about pushing the boundaries of what is possible in the human genome.

Postdoctoral Researchers

We are actively recruiting postdoctoral fellows with a strong background in molecular biology, genome editing, hematopoietic stem cell biology, or immunology. Experience with CRISPR-based tools, primary cell culture, or high-throughput functional genomics is a plus. We offer an intellectually stimulating environment, close mentorship, and strong support for career development and independence.

PhD Students

We welcome applications from motivated students interested in pursuing a PhD through the DTU PhD programme. Prospective students with backgrounds in biology, biotechnology, bioengineering, bioinformatics, or related fields are encouraged to get in touch. PhD positions are fully funded.

Master's and Exchange Students

We regularly host master's and exchange students for research projects of 4–12 months. If you are looking for a hands-on research experience in genome engineering and cell therapy, we would love to hear from you.

How to Apply Please send a CV, a brief description of your research interests and background, and the names of two references to rapfer [at] dtu.dk. Informal inquiries are very welcome.
